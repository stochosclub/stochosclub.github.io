the idea

The idea was to developing a social mobile app where you can share your goals in mental, physical, economical and social wellness and interact with experts or people that already achieved that goals. It works as a simple goals or tasks app, you set your goal and if you want you share that; sharing that it will be visible on a feed page, where experts or other people can chose to help your project/goal and interact with you by chat, videochat and so on.

After conducting original research, we have found that millennials' challenges stem from a conflict between their desires and attainable goals. Our societal expectations have created tension for millennials who struggle to find ways to act like adults when financial pressures cause them to feel much more like children.
